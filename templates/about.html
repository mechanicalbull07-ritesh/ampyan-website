About AMPYAN

An automotive ecosystem built to make ownership easier to understand.

AMPYAN combines trusted community learning, vehicle intelligence and service discovery so owners and mechanics can work from the same understanding.

Why AMPYAN exists

Modern vehicles are packed with sensors, software and systems, but the average owner still struggles to get simple, trustworthy guidance. AMPYAN is being built to remove that confusion and make vehicle decisions clearer.

The ecosystem

  • AMPYAN CommunityReal owners ask questions, share fixes and build credibility through useful answers.
  • AMPYAN IntelligenceThe intelligence layer helps interpret symptoms, explain severity and point users toward smarter next steps.
  • Garage NetworkLocal mechanics and workshops can register, build trust and become discoverable inside the same platform.

What users get

ClarityDiagnosis + context
TrustCommunity + garage reputation
ContinuityOne account across web and app vision

Founder vision

AMPYAN is created by Ritesh Singh, an automotive engineer and creator focused on making technical knowledge more practical, accessible and trustworthy for real users. The platform is being built step by step with long-term product thinking.
