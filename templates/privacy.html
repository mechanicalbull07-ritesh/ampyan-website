Privacy Policy

AMPYAN respects your privacy and is committed to handling your information responsibly.

Last Updated: 2026

AMPYAN collects only the information needed to operate your account, deliver vehicle-related features and improve the product experience.

Information We Collect

Google Login

AMPYAN may allow sign-in with Google. We only receive basic profile information such as your email address and do not access passwords, Drive, contacts or unrelated private Google data.

How We Use Your Data

  • To improve diagnostic quality and product accuracy
  • To support community and trust systems

Data Protection

We implement reasonable security measures to help protect user data and reduce unauthorized access risk. No platform can promise absolute security, but AMPYAN is designed to handle information with care.
