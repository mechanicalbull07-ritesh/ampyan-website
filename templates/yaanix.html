YAANIX.AI

YAANIX.AI represents the intelligence layer of the AMPYAN platform.

It is being developed as an evolving system designed to assist users in understanding vehicle symptoms and potential automotive issues.

Modern vehicles generate large amounts of data and complex mechanical behaviour. YAANIX.AI aims to interpret this complexity and guide users toward better understanding of their vehicles.

Rather than replacing human expertise, the system is designed to support vehicle owners by combining engineering knowledge with intelligent analysis.

Over time, intelligent automotive systems may evolve further to interact more directly with machines and mechanical systems.

YAANIX.AI is the first step toward that long-term direction.
